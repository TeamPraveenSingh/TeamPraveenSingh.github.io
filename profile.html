About Praveen Kumar Singh

A Dedicated Politician and Social Worker

Praveen Kumar Singh

Praveen Kumar Singh is a dedicated politician and social worker known for his unwavering commitment to his community. Born on October 1, 1975, he has made significant contributions to the political landscape of Sitapur, Uttar Pradesh. Throughout his career, Praveen has balanced his roles as a politician and a professional, always striving to improve the lives of those around him.

Early Life and Family Backgrounder

Praveen Kumar Singh was born in the small village of Anoghi in Sitapur, Uttar Pradesh. Coming from a family with no political background, he grew up understanding the challenges faced by ordinary citizens. His parents instilled in him the values of hard work, honesty, and social responsibility, which have been the guiding principles of his life and career.

Education

Praveen pursued his education with determination, eventually attending Lucknow University. His time at the university was marked by academic excellence and a growing awareness of social issues. His education laid a strong foundation for his future endeavors in public service, equipping him with the knowledge and skills necessary to make a meaningful impact in his community.

Interest in Politics

Praveen's interest in politics was driven by his desire to serve his community and address the issues faced by the people of his region. His grassroots activism and dedication to public welfare made him a respected figure among his peers and constituents. His commitment to social justice and development initiatives fueled his political ambitions, leading him to pursue a career in politics.

Joining SP and Block Pramukh Elections

Praveen Kumar Singh joined Samajwadi Party (SP) in the year 2009, marking the beginning of his formal political journey. His dedication and hard work quickly earned him the trust and support of his community. In 2011, he was elected as the Block Pramukh of Machhrehta in Sitapur. During his tenure, he implemented various development projects and worked tirelessly for the betterment of his constituents, establishing a reputation for honesty and integrity.

Work With PWD

In addition to his political career, Praveen is also a respected contractor with the Public Works Department (PWD). His dual roles have allowed him to contribute significantly to the infrastructure development in his region. His expertise and commitment to quality have earned him a reputation for efficiency and reliability in his professional work, further enhancing his standing in the community.

Joining BJP and Support Through Lok Sabha 2024 Elections

March 2024, Praveen Kumar Singh made a significant shift in his political alignment by joining the Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP) . Inspired by the party's vision and leadership, he saw an opportunity to further his commitment to nation-building. Praveen's decision to join the BJP underscored his dedication to serving the nation and contributing to its progress. He actively supported the BJP through the Lok Sabha 2024 elections , advocating for the party's vision and the leadership of Prime Minister Narendra Modi and Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath. His support was instrumental in garnering voter confidence and reinforcing the party's commitment to development and good governance.
