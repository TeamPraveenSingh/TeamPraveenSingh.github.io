About

Praveen Kumar Singh

Praveen Kumar Singh, an esteemed figure in his community, served as the Block Pramukh of Macharehta, Sitapur, from 2011 to 2016. During his tenure, he forged strong bonds with the public, earning their trust and respect through his dedicated service and unwavering commitment to their welfare. Recognizing the need for broader avenues to enact change and bring progress to his region, Praveen made the conscientious decision to join the Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP). Inspired by the BJP's transformative policies and impactful governance, Praveen saw an opportunity to further amplify his efforts in serving society and driving positive development. His transition to the BJP reflects his earnest desire to leverage the party's vision and resources for the betterment of his community and beyond
